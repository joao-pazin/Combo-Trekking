The expedition

Two worlds.
One journey.

Vale do Pati is considered one of the most beautiful trekking destinations in Brazil — mountain ranges, hidden waterfalls, and accommodation in locals' homes that preserve the soul of Chapada Diamantina.

Lençóis Maranhenses is a unique phenomenon on the planet: white dunes that transform into hundreds of crystal-clear lagoons between May and August. Few people get there on foot, the right way.

On this expedition you do both back-to-back, with a specialist guide, full logistics, and a small group of people who also chose the wilder path.

🌿 Chapada Diamantina – BA · 64km 🌊 Lençóis Maranhenses – MA · 32km
